Pokémon Restock Monitor Privacy Policy

Pokémon Restock Monitor does not collect, store, or process personal user data.

The app operates by monitoring automated retail alert feeds and posting restock notifications to Reddit communities where it is installed. No user-identifying information, browsing behavior, or account data is accessed or retained.

The app does not use cookies, tracking technologies, analytics, or third-party advertising services.

If you have questions about this app, contact the app maintainer via Reddit.
